Description

The Bannana is smooth and slides right into your stomach. While the Biscoff and vanilla cookies give a yummy crunch to the equation. The perfect dessert to show off your culinary skills to your family and friends or just to eat!

Ingredients!!

  • 10 bannana's (ofc!)
  • 1 pack of instant vanilla pudding and pie filling mix (like jello)
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1/2 cups of powdered sugar
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk(about 14 ounces)
  • 1 table-spoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 1 pack of vanilla wafers (the brand Manner is a good option)
  • 1 package of Biscoff cookies
  • (optional) Biscoff spread (you can use as much as you want!)

Steps

  1. Beat the pudding and pie filling mix and whole milk in a medium bowl with an electric mixer on low speed. Until the pudding and pie mix is combined and a little bit thickened(about 2 minutes). Keep it in a room temperature until the batter goes down in the bowl(about 5 minutes) (If you want, you can add a little biscoff spread)
  2. In seperate large bowl beat cream and powdered sugar with an electric mixer on medium-high speed (about 5 minutes).Carefully fold sweetened condensed milk, vannilla, and pudding mixture until everything is combined together.
  3. In seperate ziplock bags crush 5 vanilla wafers(or more/less if you want) and 4 biscoff cookies; set it aside for garnishing. Reserve 16 of the remaining vanilla wafers.
  4. Arrange another 16 of the remaining vanilla wafers and 7 of the remaining biscoff cookies in a single laye in a 41/2-quart trifle dish, alternating between the vanilla wafers and biscoff cookies. Place 21/2 cups of bannana slices in an even layer on top of the on top of the cookies. Spread 3 cups of vanilla custard into an even layer over the bananas. Then repeat the layering process 3 times with the remaining vanilla wafers, biscoff cookies, bannana slices, and vanilla custard. You then sprinkle crushed biscoff over the top layer of custard (you can add ). Then you put the 16 leftover vanilla wafers around the edge of the dish(to make your dessert look pretty!). Finally, loosly cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at leat 3 hours or at most 12 hours before you start serving your delicious masterpiece!
  5. You can make the pudding through step 2 and store it in a airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
  6. Now it's time to eat!

Nutella Filled Raspberry Chocolate Cookies üç™üç´

Description

Are you a nutella lover? If so, then this delicious dessert is perfect for you! The gooey Nutella flavor mixed with the rich raspberry and chocolate flavor is to die for. You must try them now!

Ingredients!!

  • 1/2 cup of nutella (or more if you are a nutella lover)
  • 1 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • (optional) 1/2 teaspoon of instant coffee
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted room temperature butter
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup of chocolate chips
  • (optinal)can add 1/2 cup or more of raspberries (preferably frozen raspberries)

Steps

  1. Line a small baking sheet with parchment paper. Add 12-13 teaspoons of Nutella and freeze for 30 minutes to an hour, until firm. Also, take out another small baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Wash and line out around 12-13 raspberries (more is great!). Make sure they do not touch and freeze them for around 1-2 hours, until they are firm, but not frozen solid.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350F (180C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl mix with cocoa powder, instant coffee, salt, and baking soda. Set it aside until we are ready to use it.
  4. In another bowl mix the butter with the brown and regualr sugar, until creamy. Add an egg and vanilla extract and mix until it is well incorporated with all of the other ingredients.
  5. Use a wooden spoon or spatula to incorporate the flour mixture, chocolate chips, and frozen raspberries.
  6. Remove the nutella teaspoons from the freezer. Dust your hands with cocoa powder and take large spoons of your cookie dough and flatten it on your palm. Add the frozen Nutella and wrap the dough around the Nutella and roll into balls. Place the balls onto the sheet of parchement paper you prepared earlier (in step 2).
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes and remove your nwely made cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for about 5 mintues. (If you wnat you can add an additional raspberry on top of your cookies)
  8. Now you can serve and eat them! Enjoy!

Cool Mini Ice Cream Cakes üç®üòÅ

Description

Ingredients!!

  • 2 pints of desired ice cream flavor(s)
  • Cooking spray
  • Brownie mix
  • (optinal) zest from oranges
  • (optional) 12 wafer cookies
  • 8 ounces of chocolae chips
  • 1/4 cup of coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons of sprinkles or candy

Steps

  1. (BEFORE) Follow the directions of the brownie mix to create delicious brownies (can use a bit of orange zest for a tangy taste)
  2. Let the ice cream defrost at room temperature to soften, about 10 minutes. Use a standard 12-cup muffin tin and spray the wells with cooking spray. Cut 12 sqaures of plastic tinfoil/wrap and line each well with a square shape (The cooking spray helps the plastic wrap stick to the sides).
  3. Cut the brownie lengthwise into 1/2 inch wide planks. Use a 2 1/4-inch wide cookie cutter to cut the brownie 12 times. Then, place a brownie circle in each muffin well.
  4. Spoon the ice cream from the first pint over the brownie and spread into an even layer. If using, place 1 wafer cookie in each well on top of the ice cream (don't worry if the wafers break). Scoop ice cream from the second pint ove rthe first layer of ice cream or wafer and spread into an even layer. Place the muffin tin face up in the freezer and freeze until solid, at least 1 hour.
  5. When you are ready to serve/eat make the toppings! Place the chocolate and coconut oil in a medium microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on low setting in 30-wave intervals and stir between intervals, until it is melted and smooth. (Or you can melt on a stovetop)
  6. Grab the plastic wrap around the mini ice cream cake, and remove them from the pan. Remove and discard the tinfoil from the ice cream cakes and place the cakes brownie-side up. Spoon the chocolate sauce over the top of the cake, completly covering the top and let it drip down the sides. Sprinke 1/2 teaspoon of sprinlkes on top or place candy on top. Repeat the process with the remaining cakes (work with only a few cakes because the topping sauce hardens quickly).
  7. Serve your cool creation immediately!
